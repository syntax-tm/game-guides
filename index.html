  2. Once all of the lights on the switches are green, an audio cue will play and you can head to the Power House to turn on the power. After power is restored, head to Operations and interact with Rushmore.

  1. Around the map there will be 3 newly-spawned mannequins. One of the mannequins will be glowing blue. Each player must go to the area the mannequin is in and interact with it to start a defense wave. After enough time has passed, the mannequin will break, leaving behind a part for you to pick up. Repeat this process two additional times after which you will have picked up two arms and a head.
  2. With all 3 parts in hand, bring them to the broken mannequin in the chair at APD Interrogation.
  3. Once the mannequin has been revived, Rushmore will then tell you to go find an Elemental Orb around the map. Once you find the orb, you must then escort it around the map until it gets to APD Interrogation.
    • This is a good time to use either of the slow zombie codes.
      • NOOB [6662]
      • EASY [3279]
      • Only one code of a given type can be activated per-game.
  4. Whenever you're ready, head to APD Control and have every player interact with the computer to start the boss fight.

  1. Kill mannequins around the APD Control area to fill up the empty soul canisters. Once full, the door of the APD will open, revealing the Avogadro.

  2. Once the Nuke has landed, leave the APD Control area and find the backup soul canisters around the bunker. When you find a canister you must have every player in your game go in that area and have 1 person interact with the canister to allow it to be filled with souls. While you are attempting to fill the canisters the Avogadro will attack you. Once you have filled it with enough souls an audio cue will play and then electricity will fill that room, blocking you access to it.

    Canister Locations
    Beds Storage Diner Lounge

  3. Once all 4 canisters have been filled, head back to APD Control. When Avogadro is close, start shooting him to make him move in the direction you're shooting. You must shoot him so that he gets in front of the APD door. Once he is in front he will be sucked into the APD.

  4. After securing Avogadro back in the APD, head over to the central panel to initiate the prisoner transfer.

  5. After Rushmore is done talking the APD door will open and you're able to pick up the Elemental Shard. As soon as that's done, a cutscene will play and the game will end.

  • The next round will now start with the announcer saying "Rip Flesh from Bone" and all the zombies will be replaced by mannequins that have a lot of health.

  • After surviving the wave of mannequins, a perk power-up will fall from the sky in front of the Nuketown sign in the Cul-de-sac.

  • When the Clock in the Cul-de-sac is on 1 and the Nuketown sign population number is 15 there will be a purple orb that will spawn in the shed in the Yellow House Backyard. Stand next to the orb while the number on the sign is 15 and it will start traveling. Stay close to the orb as it travels throughout the map (if you go too far away from the orb it will disappear and you will have to wait for the clock and sign to line up again). The orb will eventually reach APD Interrogation where it will go into a TV upstairs. You must now break this TV using the melee of the Bowie Knife which will then cause a code to come out of the printer to the left of the TV.
